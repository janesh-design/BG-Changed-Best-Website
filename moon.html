Analytical Chemistry

A branch of chemistry that involves the detection and estimation of chemical substance from the mixture and solution is Analytical Chemistry.

Ionic Equilibrium

The weak electrolytes mainly contains unionised molecules with few ions. In such electrolytes, equilibrium is established between ions and unionized molecules. Such equilibrium involving ions in aqueous solution is called ionic equilibrium.

Chemical Kinetics

Chemical Kinetics is the branch of physical chemistry which deals with a study of the rate of chemical reactions , factor affecting the rate of the reactions and the mechanism by which the reaction proceeds.

Thermodynamics

Thermodynamics deals with flow of heat and quantitatives relationship between heat and other forms of energy in physico-chemical transformation.It deals with changes between two states of a system but it does not provides the mechanism of how the change is accomplished.

Electrochemistry

Electrochemistry is the branch of chemistry which study of production of electrical energy released from spontaneous chemical reactions and use of electrical energy to bring non-spontantaneous chemical reactions.

Transition Elements

Tranisition elements are partly filled d-orbitals elements which form coloured compound and used in pigments for traffic lane paints, ceramic glaze and oil paints.
